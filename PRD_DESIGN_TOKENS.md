# Product Requirements Document: Design Tokens & Global Styles

You are building a high-end, responsive Product Design Portfolio website based on a provided visual layout. The theme features a premium, dark-forest green hero section transitioning into soft, clean off-white backgrounds for content.

## 1. Color Palette

### Primary & Backgrounds
* **Deep Forest Green (Hero BG):** `#032B1D` (or equivalent ultra-dark rich green)
* **Off-White Background (Content BG):** `#F4F9F5` (soft, light sage-tinted off-white)
* **Pure White (Card BG):** `#FFFFFF`
* **Dark Neutral (Text/Primary Titles):** `#111111`
* **Muted Neutral (Body text):** `#555555`

### Accents & Brand Colors
* **Vibrant Lime Green (Buttons & Highlights):** `#8CE343` (Highly saturated, energetic lime)
* **Deep Forest Accent Green (Subtle Badges):** `#0B4632`
* **Electric Orange (Decorative shapes):** `#FF6B00`

---

## 2. Typography
* **Headings:** Modern Sans-Serif (e.g., Plus Jakarta Sans, Inter, or Satoshi). Bold, clean geometric shapes.
* **Body Text:** Highly readable geometric UI sans (e.g., Inter or DM Sans).

---

## 3. Global Interactive Elements

### Buttons
1.  **Primary Lime Button:**
    * Background: `Vibrant Lime Green`
    * Text: `Deep Forest Green` or `Dark Neutral` (High contrast, bold)
    * Border-Radius: Full pill or slightly rounded rectangular (`8px` to `9999px` based on component)
    * Hover effect: Smooth scale up (1.02x) or slight brightness shift.
2.  **Secondary Outline Button (Dark BG context):**
    * Background: Transparent
    * Border: `1px solid #FFFFFF`
    * Text: `#FFFFFF`
3.  **Secondary Outline Button (Light BG context):**
    * Background: Transparent
    * Border: `1px solid #111111`
    * Text: `#111111`

### Cards
* **Container:** White background, subtle boundary padding, crisp edges, very soft border radius (`16px`).
* **Shadows:** Extremely minimal or flat layout to maintain a clean editorial feel.
